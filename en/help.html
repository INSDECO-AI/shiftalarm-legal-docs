ShiftAlarm user guide

Version 1. Date: 2026-06-23.

1. First launch

Open the app, choose the required language, check permissions and configure alarm reliability. On some devices, you may also need to allow background activity and remove battery restrictions.

2. Alarms

Create regular alarms, repeat-by-day alarms, date alarms and shift-linked alarms. For important alarms, check notifications, exact alarms and battery restrictions.

3. Geo-alarms

Create a geo-alarm on the map with a long press. For better reliability, use an 800–1000 m radius and allow precise and background location.

4. Shifts

Create an active shift schedule. Shift-linked alarms and calendar widgets use the active schedule and account for day overrides.

5. Notes and reminders

Use text notes, lists, events, reminders, audio notes and voice transcript. Offline speech models can be downloaded in settings.

6. Widgets

Add a Today Widget or Calendar Widget to the Android home screen. Widgets show nearby information and update automatically, with a manual refresh button as well.

7. Backups

Settings allow saving a backup to the phone or sharing it through the Android share sheet.

8. Diagnostics

If a feature does not work correctly, open the corresponding diagnostics log, copy or send it to the developer together with a problem description.
